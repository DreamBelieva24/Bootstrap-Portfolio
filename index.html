About Me

Greetings! Thank you for visiting my page. I am a math teacher turned developer. It's amazing how similar the two different career paths actually are. I worked as a teacher for 9.5 years. I was a virtual teacher for 5 years. Basically, I just made a switch from "math code" to computer code and from students to clients. My experience with teacher collaboration has built very strong communication skills and has made me a team player.

As for my personality, I have never seemed to fit in one "box." I am analytical but creative. I work well independently but also enjoy working as a team. I have always been logical and math minded and mix it up by being empathetic and understanding as well.

Ultimately, I believe that time is a precious thing which causes me to be very efficient and focus on building and keeping relationships. You should enjoy what you spend your time on!
